# Product Overview

## Product Purpose
Agentic Research Engineer is a CLI-based AI assistant that helps machine learning researchers convert vague research ideas into structured, reproducible experiment plans with clear hypotheses, baselines, evaluation metrics, and documentation. It bridges the gap between initial research concepts and rigorous experimental design, improving research quality and reproducibility.

## Target Users
- **Machine Learning Researchers**: Academic and industry researchers who need to design robust experiments
- **Graduate Students**: PhD and Masters students learning proper experimental methodology
- **Research Engineers**: Engineers implementing and scaling research ideas who need structured approaches

**User Needs:**
- Transform abstract ideas into concrete experiment plans
- Ensure reproducibility and proper documentation
- Follow best practices for ML experimentation
- Reduce time spent on experiment planning overhead
- Improve research quality and rigor

## Key Features
- **Idea-to-Plan Conversion**: Transform vague research concepts into structured experiment designs
- **Hypothesis Generation**: Help formulate clear, testable hypotheses from research ideas
- **Baseline Identification**: Suggest appropriate baselines and comparison methods
- **Evaluation Metrics**: Recommend relevant metrics for different types of ML problems
- **Experiment Templates**: Generate standardized experiment structures and documentation
- **Reproducibility Checklist**: Ensure all necessary components for reproducible research
- **Documentation Generation**: Auto-generate experiment documentation and README files
- **Integration Support**: Work with popular ML frameworks and experiment tracking tools

## Business Objectives
- **Improve Research Quality**: Help researchers design more rigorous and reproducible experiments
- **Accelerate Research Workflow**: Reduce time spent on experiment planning and setup
- **Educational Impact**: Teach best practices to graduate students and new researchers
- **Community Adoption**: Become a standard tool in ML research workflows
- **Research Reproducibility**: Contribute to solving the reproducibility crisis in ML research

## User Journey
1. **Input Research Idea**: User provides a vague or high-level research concept via CLI
2. **Interactive Refinement**: Tool asks clarifying questions to understand the research goal
3. **Plan Generation**: AI generates structured experiment plan with hypotheses, methods, and metrics
4. **Review and Customize**: User reviews and refines the generated plan
5. **Implementation Guidance**: Tool provides code templates and implementation suggestions
6. **Documentation Creation**: Auto-generates experiment documentation and tracking setup
7. **Execution Support**: Provides guidance during experiment execution and result analysis

## Success Criteria
- **Adoption Metrics**: Number of active users and experiments planned
- **Quality Improvement**: User feedback on experiment plan quality and completeness
- **Time Savings**: Reduction in experiment planning time reported by users
- **Reproducibility**: Percentage of experiments that can be successfully reproduced
- **Educational Impact**: Usage in academic courses and research training programs
- **Community Engagement**: GitHub stars, contributions, and community discussions
